How to Play

Welcome to the Number Guessing Game!

Here's how to play:

  • Guess a number between 1 and 100.
  • If your guess is too low, you'll receive a hint.
  • If your guess is too high, you'll also receive a hint.
  • Try to guess the number in as few attempts as possible!
  • Good luck!
